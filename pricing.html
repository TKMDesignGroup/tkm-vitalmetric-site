How plan length works

  • You subscribe monthly, starting from $5.99 / month for Basic or $9.99 / month for Plus.
  • Inside that month, you can generate 7, 14, 21, or 28 day plans – it’s up to you.
  • Longer plans get a better effective weekly price and can be split into multiple grocery runs if you prefer.
  • In the full system, subscriptions and free trials will be handled by Stripe in a secure checkout flow.

What every plan includes

Pantry-aware planning

The system is designed to start with what you already have on hand. Your pantry, fridge, and freezer become the base for your weekly plan, reducing waste and extra trips.

Real-life scheduling

Busy days, changing routines, and unexpected events are part of life. Plans are meant to flex with you, not demand perfection.

Exercise & activity integration

From gentle mobility to structured training, weekly movement targets can be woven into your meal plan so food and activity support the same direction.

Reset-friendly design

If your week goes sideways – guests, appointments, or you just change your mind – you can regenerate a new plan using updated pantry and budget info.

How billing will work in the live system

  1. You choose Basic or Plus and confirm the price on this page.
  2. You are sent to a secure Stripe Checkout page to start a free trial.
  3. During the trial, you can generate plans, test resets, and see how it fits your life.
  4. When the trial ends, Stripe starts your subscription unless you cancel.
  5. You can upgrade or downgrade between Basic and Plus from a simple account page.

Right now, this site is a UI prototype only – no real billing or account data is stored. It’s designed to show what the experience will feel like once the backend is connected.
