When life blows up your schedule

Quick-swap meal helper (concept preview)

Busy day? Doctor’s appointment moved? In the full version, you’ll tap a button, choose how many minutes you have, and get 2–3 low-sodium ideas using ingredients you already own.

Quick-swap will live inside your Weekly Snapshot. When real life changes, you won’t have to scrap your whole plan — you’ll just swap a meal or snack.

  • Choose how much time you have (3–10, 10–20 minutes, etc.).
  • Tell it what equipment you can use right now.
  • Get 2–3 realistic options that respect your sodium and calorie targets, using your actual pantry items.

In this prototype, Quick-swap is a concept only. The full version will connect to your real pantry and recipe database.
