How Wellness Pilot helps

Four pieces that make your week feel doable.

You answer 13 quick steps. We turn it into a realistic weekly plan you can actually follow.

Smart weekly meal planning

Turn your food preferences, schedule, and goals into a 7–28 day meal plan with breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks mapped out.

Pantry-aware planning

Use what you already have first. Track pantry staples, leftovers, and freezer items so less food — and money — goes to waste.

Budget and store sales

Set a weekly food budget, then add local grocery PDFs (PLUS plan) so your shopping list leans on real-world deals.

Daily movement stack

Keep it low-impact and realistic: short sessions of walking, stretching, Tai Chi, or your favorite routine — built right into the plan.
