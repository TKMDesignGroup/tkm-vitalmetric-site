QuickSwap · “I only have 10 minutes, what can I eat?”

Some days don’t go according to plan. QuickSwap is a simple helper you can use when your schedule changes and you need a fast, safer option using what you already have.

In the full system, you’ll be able to tell QuickSwap how much time you have, what equipment is available, and which items you want to prioritize from your pantry.

  • “I only have 5 minutes for lunch.”
  • “I have a microwave and toaster, but I don’t want to cook from scratch.”
  • “Use my pantry peanut butter, bread, and fruit if possible.”
