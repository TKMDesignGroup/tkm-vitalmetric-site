How it works

Four simple steps to turn your pantry, budget, and health goals into a realistic weekly plan.

Step 1 · Create your account

Start a free 7-day trial, then just $9.99/week. In the full system, your profile stays with you from week to week.

Step 2 · Complete your onboarding

Tell us about your likes, dislikes, sodium goals, budget, stores, movement preferences, and schedule — once, then adjust as needed.

Step 3 · Generate your weekly plan

The engine builds a full week of meals, snacks, movement, and a shopping list that uses your pantry before sending you to the store.

Step 4 · Follow your mission dashboard

Use the Weekly Snapshot as your control panel, make quick swaps, and keep an eye on calories, sodium, budget, and movement in one place.
