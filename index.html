How it works

  1. Answer the onboarding wizard. Share your goals, food likes/dislikes, pantry, budget, schedule, and exercise preferences.
  2. Review your weekly dashboard. See a clear snapshot of meals, snacks, movement, and your estimated grocery total.
  3. Shop your smart list. Take a grocery list that respects your budget and uses what you already have in your pantry and freezer.
  4. Reset plans when life happens. During an active subscription, you can rerun your plan as often as you like when your pantry or schedule changes.
