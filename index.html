How it works (once the full system is live)

This prototype shows the exact flow your members will use — from creating an account to reviewing their weekly mission control dashboard.

Step 1

Create your account

Sign up, confirm your email, and start a 7-day free trial so you can explore the full planner without commitment.

Step 2

Complete onboarding

Answer questions about food preferences, sodium goals, pantry, budget, stores, movement, and schedule in a guided wizard.

Step 3

Generate your weekly plan

The system selects recipes, builds a meal map, and creates your Weekly Snapshot, shopping list, and movement stack.

Step 4

Follow your mission dashboard

Keep everything in one place so you can quickly see your day, swap meals, and print an updated PDF whenever your week changes.
