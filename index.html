Prototype · UI only · No login required

Design your nutrition & movement week like a pro coach.

TKM VitalMetric helps you plan meals, sodium, budget, and movement for a full week, then turns it into a printable report you can actually follow.

  • • Weekly meal plan with low-sodium, budget-friendly recipes
  • • Smart shopping list built from your pantry and store prices
  • • Movement stack (Radio Taiso, Tai Chi, walking) calibrated to you
  • • Dashboard view of calories, sodium, budget, and trends

In the working app, this panel will be populated from your answers: pantry, dislikes, exercise level, store ads, and your weekly goals.

1 · Weekly Meal Plan

Breakfast · Lunch · Dinner · 3 snacks per day.

  • • Emphasis on lower-sodium choices vs. your old pattern
  • • Uses your pantry first to keep costs down
  • • Integrates “planned leftovers” to save time and money

2 · Smart Shopping List

Built from recipes + pantry + store prices.

  • • Flags low-sodium products where available
  • • Compares stores you care about (e.g., Aldi, Acme, Walmart)
  • • Shows estimated total and “cheapest combo” scenario

3 · Movement & Metrics

Gentle, sustainable movement + trend tracking.

  • • Daily movement stack summarized in your report
  • • Tracks calories burned vs. calories eaten (prototype)
  • • Designed to work for beginners and higher-level plans later
