Step 2 – Plan basics: dates & length

Step 7 – Stores, budget & local ads

Set your weekly budget and tell Wellness Pilot where you like to shop. PLUS members can upload store PDFs so the plan can lean on weekly sales.

This is a target, not a guarantee. The system will aim to keep recipes and grocery list within this range.

In the real PLUS version, Wellness Pilot would scan these PDFs, tag sale items, and tilt your weekly plan toward the best deals at your chosen stores. This demo doesn’t process uploads yet.

Step 13 – Confirm & generate (demo only)

In the live version, this step would summarize everything you've told Wellness Pilot and then build your weekly plan.
  • Your plan dates & length look correct.
  • Food likes, dislikes, and medical notes reflect your current reality.
  • Pantry text and store choices feel right for this week.
  • Budget range, movement stack, and schedule match what you can realistically do.

This prototype does not actually generate a real plan. In the full app, clicking the button below would:

  • Build your weekly meal & snack plan.
  • Create a grocery list aligned with your pantry.
  • Layer your daily movement stack onto each day’s schedule.
  • Produce a dashboard view and downloadable weekly report.
