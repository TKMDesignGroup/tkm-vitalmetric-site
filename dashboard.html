Weekly snapshot · Demo

This is your mission control for the week

In the real app, this card updates automatically when you regenerate your plan, tweak your pantry, or change your schedule. It keeps the most important numbers in one place.

  • 7 days of meals planned with leftovers on purpose.
  • Movement blocks scheduled around your busy days.
  • Grocery list aligned to your preferred stores.
  • Targets tuned for your calorie and sodium goals.

Meals overview

How your meals are structured

  • 7 days of breakfasts, lunches, dinners, and 1–2 snacks.
  • Leftovers are planned on purpose to reduce cooking fatigue.
  • Recipes match your time, skill level, and equipment.
  • Each day stays close to your calorie and budget targets, with room for normal life.

Nutrition & diets

How your diet style is applied

  • You choose your focus: calories, sodium, protein, fiber, or “balanced.”
  • Recipes are tagged to match those choices.
  • The planner favors pantry items you already own before adding extras.
  • In future versions, you’ll see sodium and calorie summaries per day and per week.

Budget & stores

How your grocery budget is protected

  • Plans are built against a weekly or multi-week budget you can adjust.
  • Pantry-first logic uses what you already own before adding new items.
  • In Plus plans, local ads and store preferences help prioritize good value options.
  • Shopping lists are grouped by store section to make trips faster and less stressful.

Exercise & activity

How movement fits into your week

  • You choose your baseline, from gentle recovery plans to training-style plans.
  • Movement suggestions are scheduled around your meal times and busy days.
  • Low-impact options (like walking, tai chi, or chair routines) are always available.
  • Future versions will show estimated calories burned alongside intake targets.
