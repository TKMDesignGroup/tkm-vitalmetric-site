How meals are structured

  • 7 × breakfasts, 7 × lunches, 7 × dinners.
  • Optional snacks: morning, afternoon, evening.
  • Intentional leftovers for busy days (you choose how many).
  • Mix of familiar comfort foods and lighter options.

In the real app, this panel will use your likes/dislikes, pantry, and schedule to pick meal types and leftover patterns.

How sodium is reduced

  • Swap high-sodium items (canned soup, deli meats) for lower-sodium versions.
  • Use herbs, citrus, and spices to keep flavor high while salt goes down.
  • Limit “salt bomb” meals to rare, planned treats — not surprises.
  • Highlight “green-heart” meals that are extra blood-pressure friendly.

Movement stack for the week

  • Daily gentle routine: Radio Taiso + light stretching.
  • 3–7 days of Tai Chi / Qigong for balance and calm.
  • Baseline steps: ~2,500+ per day (adjustable).
  • “Done for the day” checklist on your plan for quick wins.

Later, this panel will pull directly from your movement preferences and schedule.

Budget overview

  • Target: about $75/week (demo).
  • Heavy use of pantry items to reduce waste.
  • Store-brand swaps where it doesn’t affect taste.
  • Optional “doctor-appointment day” quick meals for emergencies.

In production, this ties into real recipe prices and your preferred stores.
