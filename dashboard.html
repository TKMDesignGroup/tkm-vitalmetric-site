How this demo dashboard is organized

Meals, sodium strategy, movement, and budget

Below are the core pieces that your future dashboard will show every week. This demo uses placeholder text so you can see the layout and flow before the full AI system is connected.

A
How the meals are structured
In the completed system, this panel would summarize how many breakfasts, lunches, dinners, and snacks are planned each day, along with how leftovers are used and where batch cooking appears.
B
How sodium is being reduced
This panel will explain your sodium strategy for the week: which high-sodium items are swapped out, how canned or packaged foods are limited, and where lower-sodium recipes are prioritized.
C
How movement is integrated
Here you’ll see how Radio Taiso, Tai Chi, and walking are distributed across the week, with an estimated calorie burn based on your current weight and time-per-day choices.
D
How budget is being respected
This panel will describe how the plan uses pantry items, store brands, and sale-friendly recipes to stay within your weekly grocery budget while still hitting your nutrition targets.
