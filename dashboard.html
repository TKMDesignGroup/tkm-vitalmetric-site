How this weekly plan works

This demo dashboard previews how your calories, sodium, meals, and movement will be organized each week.

Meals & snacks

  • 7 days of breakfasts, lunches, dinners, and snacks planned in advance.
  • Mix of pantry-based meals and fresh items tuned to your budget.
  • Leftovers used intentionally to save time and money.

Sodium strategy

  • Highlights high-sodium items so you can swap or cut back.
  • Weekly sodium focus (normal → aggressive) set during onboarding.
  • Future version: sodium score per day and per meal.

Movement & QuickSwap

  • Daily movement stack: gentle routines you can actually stick with.
  • QuickSwap™ ideas when your day doesn’t go as planned.
  • Future version: track completion, streaks, and calories burned.
