Meals overview

How your meals are structured

  • 7 days of breakfasts, lunches, dinners, and 1–2 snacks.
  • Leftovers are planned on purpose to reduce cooking fatigue.
  • Recipes are matched to your time, skill, and equipment.
  • Each day stays close to your calorie and budget targets.

Nutrition & diets

How your diet style is applied

  • You choose your focus: calories, macros, sodium, low-carb, or just “balanced.”
  • Recipes are tagged (e.g., high-protein, lower-sodium, high-fiber) to match your choices.
  • Different diet patterns (e.g., intermittent fasting, higher-protein) can be layered onto your weekly plan.
  • Each plan comes with simple, plain-English explanations for why meals were chosen.

Budget & stores

How your grocery budget is protected

  • Plans are built against a weekly or multi-week budget you control.
  • Pantry-first logic uses what you already own before adding items.
  • In Plus plans, local ads and store preferences help prioritize value options.
  • Shopping lists group items by store section to make trips faster and less stressful.

Exercise & activity

How movement fits into your week

  • You choose your baseline (from gentle recovery to training plans), and the app adjusts.
  • Movement suggestions are scheduled around meal times and your busy days.
  • Progress is shown weekly — not just “did you exercise,” but how it fits your overall plan.
  • Over time, the goal is sustainable activity that fits your real life, not perfection.

This is a prototype dashboard. It is for demonstration and UI testing only and is not medical advice. Always talk to your healthcare provider about diet and exercise changes.
